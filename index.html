The Characters of Noli Me Tangere

Character Name Description
Crisostomo Ibarra A young rich fellow who is from San Diego. Although eventually, he moved to Europe to further improve his education. He is one of the main characters in the book and the love interest of Maria Clara.
Maria Clara She is said to be the daughter of Don Santiago and is also childhood friends with Crisostomo Ibarra. She is to be wed with him, but after problems arise, the wedding was off and now she is to be wed to another.
Father Damaso He is a Spaniard in the Philippines who works as a priest. He isn't afraid to say what's in his mind, but the problem is, he says things that can be offensive towards the Philippines and it's people.
Don Santiago He is the wealthy man who hosted the one of a kind gathering in his house. He is also the father of the beautiful Maria Clara.
Don Rafael He is the wealthy father of Crisostomo Ibarra. Sadly, he died due to false accusation of killing a Spaniard. His life ended by being thrown in jail.
